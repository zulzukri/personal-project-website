VOLUNTEER EXPERIENCE

I used to volunteer in the OPKIM (Operasi Khidmat Masyarakt) program. The program is organized by Kelab Pembimbing Efektif Rakan Sebaya(PEERS). This program takes place at Desa Alam Shah, Pasir Puteh, Kelantan. The program lasted for 2 days. Among the activities carried out is gotong-royong to clean the surrounding area of Desa Alam Shah, recreational activities with residents, learning activities with children and motivational sessions with parents and children. It is a meaningful experience for me because through this program, it can give joy and smile to the residents there.
